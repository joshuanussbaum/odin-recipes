High Protein/High Fiber Waffles

This waffle recipe is packed with extra protein and fiber; perfect for indulgent breakfasts with less guilt!

Don't have a waffle maker? Skip the last three steps and pour silver dollar-sized dollops onto a buttered skillet over medium heat. Wait until bubbles form and pop on top side and bottom of pancakes come off the skillet easily, then flip and cook until both sides are a golden brown color - approximately 3 minutes per side.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams All Purpose Flour
  • 40 grams Whey Protein Powder
  • 10 grams Fiber Powder
  • 2 grams Salt
  • 13 grams White Sugar (or Sugar/Sucralose Blend)
  • 1 stick Butter, Melted
  • 400 mL whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs

Directions

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl and whisk together.
  2. Whisk eggs until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add vanilla extract to eggs and mix together.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredient mixture to eggs and gently mix with spatula to avoid breadlike texture.
  5. Add melted butter to mixture and mix gently.
  6. Add milk to mixture and mix gently.
  7. Preheat waffle iron, while resting batter for 5 minutes.
  8. Spray waffle iron with cooking spray or oil up with a stick of butter.
  9. Add mix to waffle iron, taking care not to overfill, and cook (approximately 5 minutes). Remove from iron and serve immediately with a slice of butter, maple syrup, or whatever toppings you like!
